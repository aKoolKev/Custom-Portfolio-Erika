Who Am I?

Erika Napier

"Hi there! What you find below makes up the person I am today! Nice to meet you!"

Fun Facts

I love cats! I currently have five cats: Frankie, River, Azula, Garden, and Sappy...

...I also have a German Shepherd named Amaya!

I am an artist and an art historian. My favorite art movement is Dada!

  • I have lived in California, Washington, Connecticut, and Oklahoma. I have also been out of the country: Japan.
  • I am half Filipino!
  • In the summer of 2024, I opened an online Depot store to give old clothes new homes, promoting reusability.

HOBBIES

I enjoy charcoal drawing, sketching, painting, and making ceramics. My favorite of them all is COLLAGING.

  • Creative story writing

    "I am currently working on a novel about a young veterinary technician and a newly graduated dental hygienist, both facing newfound responsibilities as they navigate the challenges of World War I in Europe.

  • Thifting

    My earliest memory of thrifting was going to a thrift store back in Washington with my mom and finding Barbies in plastic bags. It is a part of me now, and you can find treasure for a fraction of the price! Although it is cost-effective, I am all for sustainability.

  • Reading

    I am actively building up my library for my dream house in the future!

Goals

  • After obtaining my degree in Art History, I want to build up my experiences to one day work at well-known art institutions such as the St. Louis Art Museum (SLAM), the Metropolitan Museum of Art (MET), or the Art Institute of Chicago.
  • I enjoy traveling and hope to travel the world. For now, I would love to explore all of Europe and visit my mother's home country, the Philippines.
  • I want to finish the short novel I am working on and share it with others!
